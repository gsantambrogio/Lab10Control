# Description
Precilaser used to drive the [[X (v=2) -- A (v=1)]] transition after SFG with [[Precilaser 1560nm 2]]. After SFG, the produced wavelength is 628.1 nm.
The output of this laser is modulated with an EOM to match exactly the [[X (v=2) -- A (v=1)]] transition frequency after SFG. 


# Model
Precilaser FL-SF-1051-S

# Specifications
- Output power 1: 14 mW 
- Output power 2: 10 mW (for locking)
- Wavelength: 1051.22 nm
- Tuning range: 0.7 nm
- Fast frequency tuning range: 5 kHz
