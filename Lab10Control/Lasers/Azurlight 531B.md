# Description
Laser driving the [[X (v=0) -- B (v=0)]] transition

# Model
Azurlight: ALS GR 531 10 A CP SF

## Serial number 
SN00914

# Specification
Output power 10 W at 531 nm
# Connected to
Seeded by [[NKT photonics laser]]

# Locking
The amplified light of the seed [[NKT photonics laser]], Output 1, is beaten with the comb on the koheron differential photidiode, which is powered by the [[PowerSupply Funk PWS-05B-T]] power supply. The signal is amplified by a [[Amplifier ZFL-500LN+]]

Reference Local oscillators  - LO1 (Siglent SDG1062X 60MHz Function)

Servo units - Vescent2 (Power supplies for the servo - funk3 (PWS-08MP-T.V2  +5 V — 3000 mA ) and funk4 (PWS-06B-T +/- 15 V — 800 mA) )
