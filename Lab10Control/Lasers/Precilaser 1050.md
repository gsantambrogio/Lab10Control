# Description
Precilaser used for the [[X (v=3) -- A (v=2)]] transition after SFG with [[Precilaser 1560nm 1]]. After SFG, the produced wavelength is 627.7 nm.
The output of this laser is modulated with an EOM to match exactly the [[X (v=3) -- A (v=2)]] transition frequency after SFG. 
# Model
Precilaser FL-SF-1050-S

# Specifications
- Output power 1: 24 mW 
- Output power 2: 10 mW (for locking)
- Wavelength: 1050.1 nm
- Tuning range: 0.7 nm
- Fast frequency tuning range: 5 kHz
