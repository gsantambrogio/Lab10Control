# Description
Seed laser for the [[Azurlight 531B]]

For the modulation and the locking of the laser a circuit is used. The circuit is powered by a 12 V DC power supply. To enable the modulation, remember to turn on the modulation from the software.

Output power of the NKT laser is around 30 mW. This laser is further amplified by a second amplifier, pumped by the [[Toptica 1062 Pump]].  The amplifier has two output ports. One part (40mW) is used for the locking and monitoring of the laser while the second part (90 mW) goes to the main laser amplifier via a fiber EOM which generates the sideband.
