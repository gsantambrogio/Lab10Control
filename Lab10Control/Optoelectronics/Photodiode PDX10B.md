# Description
Differential InGaAs photodiode, custom made by Koheron. The product is developed by replacing the two Si photodiodes with InGaAs ones and adapting the electronics. 

# Model
PDX10B-3-DC-INGAAS
Reference: PDX10B-3-DC-SI-CUSTOM VERSION

# Specifications
Transimpedance gain 3kV/A
400 Am InGaAs photodiodes

# Serial numbers
14288, 17288, 14287, 14289

# Powered by 
[[PowerSupply Funk PWS-05B-T]]
