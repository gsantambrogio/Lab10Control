# Description
The SA30 is a high finesse Spectrum Analyzer used to examine the fine structures of the spectral characteristics of CW lasers. The spectrum analyzer consists of a near-confocal cavity that contains two high reflectivity mirrors. By varying the mirror separation with a piezoelectric transducer, the cavity acts as a very narrow bandpass filter. Knowing the free spectral range of the SA30 allows the time-base of an oscilloscope to be calibrated to facilitate quantitative measurements of a laser line shape. With a resolution of below 1 MHz, the fine structure resulting from multiple longitudinal modes of a laser line can be resolved.

# Model 
SA30-95

# Specifications
- Free spectral range: 1.5 GHz
- Finesse: 1500
- Resolution: 1 MHz
- Wavelength range: 824 -- 1071 nm
